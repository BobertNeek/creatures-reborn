# Evolution And Natural Selection

Normal gameplay uses natural selection rather than a hidden global viability score. Creatures live, breed, fail, die, or produce stillborn offspring under the same biology and safety rules.

The evolution journal records birth, stillbirth, death, reproduction, crossover, mutation, egg hatch, and survival metric frames. These records observe selection pressure; they do not force survival.

Lineage preserves weak organisms and stillborn outcomes so mutation evidence is not erased. This lets later ecology and training tools learn from both successful and failed branches.
